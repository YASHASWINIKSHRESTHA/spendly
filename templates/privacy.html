Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect only the information necessary to provide the Service:

  • Account information — your name and email address provided at registration.
  • Expense data — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter.
  • Usage data — basic logs such as login timestamps to maintain account security.

We do not collect payment information, location data, or any data beyond what you explicitly provide.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to operate and improve Spendly. Specifically, we use it to:

  • Display your expenses, summaries, and spending breakdowns within the app.
  • Authenticate your account and keep your session secure.
  • Identify and fix bugs or performance issues in the Service.

We never sell, rent, or share your personal or financial data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored in a secured database accessible only to the Service. Passwords are hashed before storage and are never stored in plain text. We take reasonable technical measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, or deletion. However, no method of storage or transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly loads fonts from Google Fonts, which may set cookies or log your IP address in accordance with Google's own privacy policy. Beyond this, we do not integrate with third-party analytics, advertising networks, or external APIs that receive your personal data. Any future integrations will be disclosed in an updated version of this policy.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding your data or this Privacy Policy, please reach out to us. We aim to respond to all privacy-related inquiries within 7 business days. You can contact us via the support link in the app or by emailing the address on file for your account.
